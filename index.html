About me

Scott Williams is a Customer Service Supervisor who partners with the CEO and managers to from many departments at a high end rug company. He relocated from NJ after working for a non profit organiztion that specialized in care of individuals with developmental disablities. He began as a shift supervisor in 2013 and was promoted twice. His first promotion was to an Assistant MAanager and then promoted to a Residence manager. As a manager he supervised 25 staff and 11 residents in Yonkers, NY. Scott became a CPR/First aid trainer and was reponsible for training 100 staff and supervisors. He was alos part of the Sanctuary support group that trained staff on how to balance work and life. He used some of his training techniques to balance his on work life ratio. Commuting to NY everday made it a challange to spend time with his wife and kids but he was disciplined in keep a balance. Scott has a B.A. in Psychology at Dominican College in Orangeburg, NY
